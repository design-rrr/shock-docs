02
Color System

Palette

Each product has a designated color identity that shifts between dark and light themes. The comparison swatches below always show both values: left half is the dark-theme color, right half is the light-theme color. The active-theme live view at the bottom updates as you switch themes.

⚡
Product colors are theme-specific — never interchangeable

Dark-theme colors are tuned for maximum luminosity on dark surfaces. Light-theme values are adjusted for legibility and reduced glare on white backgrounds. Using dark values on light backgrounds (or vice versa) violates brand guidelines.
